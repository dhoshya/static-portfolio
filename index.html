About Me

I am a Graduate Student at George Mason University majoring in Software Engineering based in Fairfax, VA.

I have been a Computer Science student since my junior high school. I pursued my passion as a Bachelor's Degree in the same and right now stated to gradute with my Master's degree in December 2018.

The focus is always on to create, innovate and develop high quality code, which has real-world applications by providing a great user friendly interface. Along with that, I am diving in the ubiquitous field of Artificial Intelligence as a part of my post-graduation.

I am lucky enough to have hands-on experience with the latest tech in the market at all points in my career. I was introduced to Image Recognition and Web Applications in the year 2014 as a part of my internship which has left me impacted since.

I am very interested towards perfecting myself as a Full-Stack Engineer and am continuously improving my skillset both as a part of my academics and personal projects.
