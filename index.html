About Me

I am a graduate from George Mason University with my Masters in Software Engineering, currently working as a Software Engineer at Global Data Consortium.
A returning board member of ISAB at George Mason University. The International Student Advisory Board (ISAB) informs, advises the Office of International Programs and Services (OIPS) at George Mason University and aims to better the lives of international students by providing feedback, challenging current policies, and designing new initiatives.

My focus is always to create, innovate and develop high quality code, which has real-world applications by providing a great user friendly interface. The intent is to be a part of a change that is impacting the daily life of people around the world. The past couple of years at GDC have given a me a lot of scope to perfect myself as a Full-Stack Engineer.

I got opportunities to create AI / ML projects in my graduate course and working with Monetate/Kibo, I was exposed to high-quality solutions for the e-Commerce businesses. Now at GDC, I am responsible for the internal-tool which is used by people to maintain, create and generate reports and perform analytics on the data available to the company.

Apart from my interests in the technological world, I like to think about myself as an extremely grateful person who appreciates everthing life presents to me. I am very passionate about learning more in the domains of investing, management, finance, psychology and music.
